Our Offerings : CFS online banking services include funds transfers between the customer's linked accounts, paying third parties, including bill payments and third party fund transfers, investment purchase or sale, loan applications and transactions, such as repayments of enrollments, credit card applications, register utility billers and make bill payments

Manage your account : CFS online banking services provides wide variety of instruments to manage your account: viewing account balances, viewing recent transactions, downloading bank statements, for example in PDF format, viewing images of paid cheques, ordering cheque books, download periodic account statements. CFS also provides two powerful instruments for checks operations and deposit operations:

  • eChecking: CFS makes it easy. Just fill in the eApplication form send us an initial deposit – any amount over $100, and volia!, you are the proud owner of a CFS checking account. You can send eChecks, send and receive PayPal, wire transfers, and yes, even paper checks. The best part is that CFS eChecking is totally free!
  • eSavings: CFS also make is very easy. Fill in the eApplication, send a deposit, and set up a transfer between eChecking and eSavings and you are in business! We offer attractive interest rates, and the convenience of banking from anywhere at anytime it suits your needs.
