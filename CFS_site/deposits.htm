Deposit services

We offer you the benefit of making time deposits on favorable terms securing a high yield on your safe investments. Sign up for an CFS Bank account with no minimum opening deposit, and when you are ready to start earning great rates, funding your account is easy.You can make an unlimited number of USD and foreign currency deposits from your current or savings account with CFS services, thus using surplus funds to your advantage. We are transparent in communicating the rates and charges of our bank on deposits. Please do visit this page often since these rates are subject to periodic revision.

You can transfer funds at any time using your computer or mobile device.

  • Move funds between your CFS Bank accounts for free
  • Make free online transfers between your CFS Bank and non-CFS Bank accounts
  • Use the direct deposit form to set up your paycheck or Social Security deposits
  • Use a wire transfer: we wont charge you to wire funds in, but check with your other bank for any fees they may charge to wire funds out
  • Send a check using free postage-paid envelopes available through online banking
